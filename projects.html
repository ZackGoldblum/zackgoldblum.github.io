Current Projects

2021-Present

BrainTech Podcast

I host and produce the BrainTech podcast. Through conversations with researchers, engineers, business leaders, and thinkers in brain technology, I hope to inspire students who will shape the future of this field.


Check it out!

Collaborated with students from UMN to create a mobile for Parkinson's and Essential Tremor patients that uses machine learning to analyze and quantify symptom severity. We leveraged smartphone data streams including accelerometer, gyroscopic, and keystroke to train the machine learning model and create visualizations for clinicians to remotely monitor disease progression over time. I coded data-acquisition aspects of the app, designed and programmed the UI, and built the data pipeline from the mobile app to cloud storage on Google Firebase.

TrachTalk | Drexel University

This software acquires 8-channel EEG data from the OpenBCI Ultracortex Mark IV headset, creates left vs. right hand motor imagery datasets, and classifies user intention in near real-time.
